Welcome to BANH MI 2U!

BanhMi2U is a chain of over twenty Vietnamese bread shops in the CBD and around Sydney.

"Banh Mi" is a popular Vietnamese baguette roll stuffed with butter, pâté, and a range of fillings and topped with herbs, cucumber slices, and pickled veggies.

In addition to bread rolls, we also offer other Asian cuisines such as dumplings and noodle soup.
